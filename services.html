Frequently Asked Questions

Should I Use Supplements?
The first step towards fitness is healthy eating. Beginners and professionals alike need a consistent healthy diet. However, supplements are consumed to provide additional protein, amino acids, and other nutrients.
What Can I do to Lose Fat?
The first step is to discard junk food and other unhealthy food that contain high levels of fats. Eating healthy, and regular exercise will lead to fat loss. The process can be facilitated by activities such as walking, outdoor running, and weight lighting.
How Often do I have to train?
Training depends on your availability, but you should make an effort to train at least three times in a week. Every session should last for 45 to 90 minutes to avoid overtraining. However, people seeking to train everyday can do so.
